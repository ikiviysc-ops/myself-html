Frequently Asked Questions

Does it impact performance? +

As with any custom code, the overhead is just from the code itself. Everything is bundled, minified, and compressed to minimize impact, avoiding external libraries and reducing CDN script count.

Where are my files hosted? +

After the build step, all bundled files are uploaded to Cloudflare R2 and served through a CDN link for fast, reliable delivery.

Help! I pushed the wrong files! +

In the deploy panel, there's an instant rollback option. Hit that to restore the previous deployment, fix locally, and push again.

What AIs are available? +

We use all major models including OpenAI, Claude, and Gemini. The system automatically orchestrates based on your needs, and you can switch to specific models manually.
